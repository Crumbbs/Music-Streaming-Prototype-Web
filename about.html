Our Story

Willify was founded in 2012 by two friends. They met at a music festival and bonded over their shared love of music. They were both frustrated with the existing music streaming services and saw an opportunity to create a better platform. They wanted to build a service that would prioritize artist compensation and provide a more personalized and community-driven experience. They also aimed to create a platform that would be inclusive of independent artists and offer a diverse range of content. They set out to build a team of passionate music lovers and experienced technologists. They launched Willify in 2016 to critical acclaim and quickly gained a dedicated user base. Since then, Willify has continued to grow and evolve, adding new features, expanding its content library, and building a vibrant community of music lovers.
